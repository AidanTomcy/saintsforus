---
title: St. Francis de Sales
lifetime: 21 August 1567-28 December 1622
---

St. Francis de Sales was the Bishop of Geneva and is honoured as a
saint in the Catholic Church. He became noted for his deep faith and gentle approach to the religious
divisions in his land resulting from the Protestant Reformation. He is known also for his writings on
the topic of spiritual direction and spiritual formation, particularly the Introduction to the Devout
Life and the Treatise on the Love of God. Francis de Sales was born on 21 August 1567 in the Chateau
de Sales into the noble Sales family of the Duchy of Savoy in what is today Thorens-Glieres, Haute-Savoie,
France.
